ABOUT US

Jujafarm Vocational Training Center is a training institute under the county government of Kiambu department of education, gender,culture and social service.We operate in compliance with national training standards and prepare our trainees for assessments conducted by the National Industrial Training Authority(NITA). Through this alignment, our learners are evaluated using recognized competency-based examinations that enhance their employability and professional credibility. We are committed to providing quality training, integrity and continuous learner support in preparation for nationally recognized certification.
